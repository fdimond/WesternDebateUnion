Interested in Debate?

Come to our meeting, every Monday at 4:00pm in room 211 of the Communications Facility.

WDU is open to anyone, no matter your experience with debate. Whether you're coming from 4 years of policy debate in high school or only just heard of this thing called debate, we think everyone should have the opportunity to debate. Come check it out.

Feel free to email Steve Woods, our Director of Forensics, for more information.
